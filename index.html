AI Tools Mastery - 12 Week Curriculum

Week 1: Basics of Generative AI & AI Tools

Overview of Gen AI, differences from traditional AI, introduction to Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini

Week 2: Machine Learning Fundamentals

Supervised vs unsupervised learning, model evaluation, and bias in AI

Week 3: Deep Learning Basics

Neural networks, transformers, and how they power modern AI systems

Week 4: Prompt Engineering Fundamentals

Writing effective prompts for better results; single-turn vs multi-turn prompts

Week 5: Agentic AI & Multi-Tool Workflows

Understanding AI agents, chaining tools together, and automating complex tasks

Week 6: Text-to-Image & Multimedia Generation

Creating visual content, editing images, and using AI for design

Week 7: Text-to-Video & Audio Generation

Producing videos, music, and realistic voiceovers with AI

Week 8: AI Ethics & Safety

Identifying misinformation, detecting deepfakes, and responsible AI usage

Week 9: AI for Research & Knowledge Work

Using AI to search, summarize, and synthesize information efficiently

Week 10: AI in Industries

How AI is transforming marketing, healthcare, education, and creative industries

Week 11: Portfolio Assembly

Organizing and polishing outputs into a cohesive creative portfolio

Week 12: Final Presentation

Sharing and reviewing completed projects

AI Engineering - 14 Week Curriculum

Week 1: Python Setup & Intro to ML

Development environment setup, Python basics, and ML concepts

Week 2: APIs & Data Ingestion

Pulling real-time and batch data from APIs and external sources

Week 3: Backend with FastAPI

Building API endpoints for AI-powered features

Week 4: Database Management with PostgreSQL

Structuring and storing application data

Week 5: Vector Search & Embeddings

Understanding embeddings, similarity search, and using vector databases

Week 6: Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) Pipeline

Integrating AI models with retrieval to answer questions from custom data

Week 7: Frontend with React

Designing a user interface for your AI app

Week 8: Full-Stack Integration

Connecting frontend, backend, and AI services

Week 9: Containerization (Docker)

Packaging applications for portability

Week 10: Cloud Deployment (Kubernetes)

Deploying and managing services in the cloud

Week 11: AWS AIOps Integration

Automating AI operations: monitoring, anomaly detection, and performance optimization

Week 12: Security & Authentication

Protecting your app and managing user access

Week 13: Scaling & Optimization

Performance tuning, autoscaling, and cost management

Week 14: Final Project Launch & Presentation

Deploy and demo your full AI application
